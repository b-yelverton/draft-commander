- Rows 50+ skip animation entirely (they're likely off-screen, saves GPU)
- The existing `@media(prefers-reduced-motion:reduce)` global rule on line 437-438 already covers this — it sets `animation-duration:.01ms!important` on all elements, so `.val-hydrated` animation is effectively instant. No additional reduced-motion rule needed.
